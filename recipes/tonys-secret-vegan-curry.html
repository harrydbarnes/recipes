Tony's Original Secret Vegan Curry Recipe

Una ricetta segreta della tradizione vegana italiana

8 people
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cooking Time: 2 hours 15 minutes
Serves: 8 people
❦ ❦ ❦

Ingredienti

Preparazione

  1. Soak 200g of red lentils in 200ml of water overnight.

  2. Dice the red bell peppers into 2cm squares and chop the cauliflower into florets smaller than 3cm. Toss the diced peppers in a little oil and roast them in an oven preheated to 200°C for 15-20 minutes until roasted.

  3. Finely chop the onions and chop the 4 cloves of garlic. Grate a 1-inch piece of ginger.

  4. Heat 95ml of oil in a pan and fry the garlic until brown, for approximately 1 minute. Add the onions, 3/4 tsp of salt, 4 cinnamon sticks, and the bay leaf, and cook until the onions are soft.

  5. Once the onions are soft, add the grated ginger, 2 tsp of Madras powder, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp ground cumin, 1 tsp ground coriander, 1/2 tsp garam masala, and a pinch of chili powder. Cook for about 30 minutes on a medium to low heat.

  6. Add a tin of chopped tomatoes and cook on medium/low heat for 30 minutes until the oil rises to the top, stirring regularly to prevent sticking.

  7. Add 650ml of water, 200g of tomato puree, and 2 tsp of sugar to the curry base.

  8. Add the soaked lentils and the cauliflower florets. Simmer for 15 minutes until the lentils have a slight bite.

  9. Finally, add the chickpeas, the roasted peppers, and the baby spinach. Bring back to a simmer.

  10. Garnish with fresh coriander if desired and serve hot.
